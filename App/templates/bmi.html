BMI Categories

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight. Here's how the categories work:

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5 - Consider consulting a healthcare professional.
  • Normal weight: BMI between 18.5 and 24.9 - Generally considered a healthy range.
  • Overweight: BMI between 25 and 29.9 - May indicate the need to consider weight loss.
  • Obese: BMI of 30 or greater - Considered a higher risk for various health issues.

It's important to note that BMI is a screening tool and does not directly measure body fat or overall health. Individual circumstances and other factors should also be considered.
